Unlabeled Data in Machine Learning

Slide 1

What is Unlabeled Data?

  • Data without predefined labels
  • No correct output provided
  • Used in unsupervised learning
Example: Customer data without categories
Slide 2

Why Use Unlabeled Data?

  • Large amounts available
  • Low cost (no labeling needed)
  • Helps discover hidden patterns
